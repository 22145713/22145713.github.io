Celtic Games surprised fans by releasing some unusual titles in recent years. One of them, “Sheep Accountant 2024”, lets players balance the finances of a magical flock of sheep, while “Potion Delivery Express” has you racing through enchanted forests to deliver unstable potions before they explode. They also launched “Kilt Runner”, a quirky endless runner where players dodge rogue bagpipes and leaping haggis, and “Mystic Tea Time”, a calming puzzle game where arranging tea cups perfectly can unlock ancient druid secrets. None of these were typical action games, but they quickly gained a cult following for their charm and humor.

1. Runestone Raiders

Genre: Adventure / Puzzle / Action

Concept: Players explore ancient Celtic ruins to recover magical runestones that hold powerful abilities. The world is full of forests, stone circles, caves, and abandoned villages.

Gameplay: Explore maps and discover hidden runestones, ach runestone gives a special power (teleport, lightning strike, invisibility, etc.), solve environmental puzzles using the powers you collect, and fight mythological creatures like giant wolves, spirits, and stone guardians.

2. Skyforce Racers

Genre: Sci-Fi Racing

Concept: A high-speed racing game where players pilot customizable hover vehicles in futuristic cities floating in the sky.

Gameplay: Race through sky tracks, floating islands, and energy tunnels, use boosts, shields, and gadgets to beat opponents, and upgrade your racer with faster engines and special abilities.

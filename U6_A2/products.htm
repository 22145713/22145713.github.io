Celtic Games surprised fans by releasing some unusual titles in recent years. One of them, “Sheep Accountant 2024”, lets players balance the finances of a magical flock of sheep, while “Potion Delivery Express” has you racing through enchanted forests to deliver unstable potions before they explode. They also launched “Kilt Runner”, a quirky endless runner where players dodge rogue bagpipes and leaping haggis, and “Mystic Tea Time”, a calming puzzle game where arranging tea cups perfectly can unlock ancient druid secrets. None of these were typical action games, but they quickly gained a cult following for their charm and humor.
